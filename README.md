**Taroudant** is a walled city in Morocco's Souss Valley, nestled between the High Atlas and Anti-Atlas mountains. Its iconic **ochre ramparts**, stretching over **7.5 kilometers**, date back to the **16th century Saadian dynasty**. The city is home to mosques, fountains, souks, and gates вҖ” all classified as irreplaceable cultural heritage.

Yet these structures are **aging**. Cracks form. Walls erode. Foundations weaken. And until now, there has been **no automated system** to detect, score, and alert authorities about the structural degradation of these monuments.

**That is the problem Taroudant Heritage Shield solves.**

## рҹ§ұ SQL Business Logic

### вҡҷпёҸ Stored Procedure 1 вҖ” `CalculateVulnerabilityScore`

```sql
-- Automatically called by Trigger 1 after each crack is logged
-- Weighted formula: crack severity Г— age multiplier Г— category factor
-- Step 1 вҖ” Crack severity weights (length-adjusted)
--   minor:    1  Г— min(length_cm / 10,  2)
--   moderate: 3  Г— min(length_cm / 20,  3)
--   major:    7  Г— min(length_cm / 30,  4)
--   critical: 15 (always maximum, length irrelevant)
-- Step 2 вҖ” Age multiplier: 1.0 + min(age_years / 1000, 0.5)
--   вҶ’ 1.0 for new structures, up to 1.5 for 500+ year old ones
--   вҶ’ applied only when cracks exist (old monument with no cracks = LOW)
-- Step 3 вҖ” Age score: min(age_years / 20, 10)  [capped at 10]
-- Step 4 вҖ” Category durability factor
--   Rempart 0.8 | Porte de ville 0.9 | Kasbah 0.85
--   MosquГ©e 1.0 | Maison traditionnelle 1.2 | Jardin 1.1
-- Step 5 вҖ” Final score
--   crack_score  = raw_crack_score Г— age_multiplier
--   total_score  = FLOOR((crack_score + age_score) Г— category_factor)
-- Override: any critical crack forces minimum HIGH risk
CALL CalculateVulnerabilityScore(inspection_id);
-- Output: INSERT into vulnerability_scores
```

**Risk level thresholds (post-formula):**

| Score Range | Risk Level |
|---|---|
| 0 вҖ“ 15 | рҹҹў LOW |
| 16 вҖ“ 35 | рҹҹЎ MEDIUM |
| 36 вҖ“ 60 | рҹҹ  HIGH |
| 61+ | рҹ”ҙ CRITICAL |

```sql
-- Output: INSERT into reports (encrypted_content, risk_level, total_score)

```
